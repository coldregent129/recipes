Jamaican Beef Patties

home

Description

When you're playing outdoor games and need a snack, these beef patties do just the trick. These handheld pastries are stuffed with savory ground beef and spices for a flavorful kick found even in the dough.

It's also a good after-party snack for when you are craving a little more.

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 1 egg, beaten
  1. 1 hr 10 mins
  2. Prep Time: 30 mins
  3. servings: 10
