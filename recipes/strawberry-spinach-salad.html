Strawberry Spinach Salad

home

Description

For most of the country, June is when strawberries are in their peak season, and it's time to start incorporating strawberries in all our recipes. This salad balances sweet and savory.

The homemade dressing can be tossed over the salad a few minutes before serving, making it a great make-ahead dish and easy to carry for a potluck gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup almods, slivered
  1. Make dressing: Whisk with sugar, oil, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, onion
  2. Make salad: combine strawberries, spinach, and almonds in a large bowl
  3. Pour dressing over salad, toss to coat. Refrigerate for 10-15 minutes before serving
